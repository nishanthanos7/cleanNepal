The Pollution Crisis: A Closer Look at Nepal 🔍

Air Pollution 💨

Air pollution in Nepal is one of the leading causes of health issues, especially in urban areas like Kathmandu. The pollution level here often exceeds WHO's safety standards.

Water Pollution 💧

Many rivers face contamination from industrial and domestic waste, affecting both human health and aquatic ecosystems.

Land Pollution 🏞️

Improper waste management leads to soil degradation and affects agricultural productivity.
